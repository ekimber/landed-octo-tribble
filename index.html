Landed Festival 2016

April 29th, 30th, May 1st

Landed Festival returns in 2016 for the Beltane weekend celebrations!

This year we're bigger & better, with more bands, more stages, and a great selection of traders, crafts and entertainments for all ages. We really want to make things better for everyone this year, and most especially you! So we've lowered the ticket prices, improved the site layout, and added two new stages.

Buy Early-bird tickets now available!

What's On

Five stages of live music and performance, including

  • The Grubby Stage – Landed's main stage. Lineup announcements to follow soon.
  • Marv's Bar Stage with a variety of acoustic acts and bands.
  • Listen That – our spoken word, poetry & music venue.
  • New The Busking Stage. Our very own Tina & Doogal will be hosting our open mic stage with the Bareroots Project from Llanfyllin, Wales.
  • New Dance area in The Spinney, our new secret dance area in the trees.

Contact Us

If you are a musician, artist, performer, or trader we'd love to hear from you.

Landed Festival welcomes a diverse range of creative people, including theatrical performers, poets, circus performers, healing area with massage and holistic therapies, crafts, and various kinds of traders and caterers. There's much more to us than just stages and music. If you would like to be a part of this inspiring 3 day event in the beautiful Welsh countryside, please get in touch now. You can apply directly with our application form or if you have any questions at all, please feel free to send us an email at applications@landedfestival.co.uk. Busking applications can also be sent to applications@landedfestival.co.uk

If you'd like to help out with stewarding, get in touch here: stewards@landedfestival.co.uk, and for any other enquiries please use enquiries@landedfestival.co.uk
